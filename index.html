An Instructional Designers Worst Nightmare

You work for a company selling coffee machines. Sales numbers have been stagnant for the past few months and have even begun to decline.

You received an email from the Sales Director, who asked you to create an e-learning program for her team to improve their skills.

She also attached an 80-page PDF containing information about their coffee machines and details of the sales conversation they are expected to have.

After some pushing, you found time on your shared calendars before you started developing the course.

Try to ask her as much as possible to create a successful course.
